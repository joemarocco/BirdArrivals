\f3\b\fs45\fsmilli22667 \cf0 Why I started this project
\f4\fs36 \
\pard\pardeftab720\sa320\partightenfactor0

\f1\b0\fs29\fsmilli14667 \cf0 In the northern Adirondacks, where I live, early spring is a fragile, shifting season: one day is bright and warm - almost short-sleeve weather; the next the temperatures drop and a spring nor\'92easter hits. The arrival of the robins brings hope that the snow will finally start to melt and winter will soon end.
\f2\fs24 \

\f1\fs29\fsmilli14667 Over the years I began to wonder whether climate change was having a measurable impact on bird arrival dates. Were robins actually arriving earlier? Were snow conditions shifting in ways I could detect? And what about other migratory species that return first to my area every spring to breed?
\f2\fs24 \

\f1\fs29\fsmilli14667 This project had its genesis in that curiosity.
\f2\fs24 \

\f1\fs29\fsmilli14667 It\'92s not meant to be a definitive scientific paper. It\'92s a personal exploration \'97 a blend of data analysis, local natural history, and a desire to better understand the place where I live.
